The Challenge

As for today, small & medium private jet operators struggle due to lack of optimisation, and demand that doesn't meet supply.
Over then 90% of jets are grounded most of the time, and many planes fly empty, in order to get to the customers location.

Huge waste of money.

Today, person who wish to fly on a private jet, needs to spend hours on research, and review a lot of quotes before he can even make a decision.

The Solution

A marketplace where flyers will connect with operators directly.
Flyer that wish to book a flight, will get real time pricing for his flight, and and instant booking.

Something like having Uber in the private jet market.
‍
In order to get social validation, flyers will be able to read reviews about every aircraft, see photos and services that each operator offers, so they can compare their options quickly.

The Results

An app that allows users to book a flight in less then 5 steps, that's even easier than booking a commercial flight.

On the other side, operators having their dashboard, when they can view their flights, post new flights they wish to do, and to stop wasting money on grounded planes, or empty flights.
