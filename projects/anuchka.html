The Challenge

Anuchka is growing fast. Every year it offers more new services, and products.
However, they had no marketing plan, and didn’t used any managing software. Actually, the first I saw haw they handling, It felt a little bit like a time travel.  
All the documents, forms, client information, was printed out, filled by hand and archived in binders, appointments were written in a simple Google Cal and the secretaries sent manually SMS reminders to clients.

Due the lack of optimisation, Anuchka could have only two rooms working at a time. This business needed full digital transformation, and needed it fast.

The Solution

First thing needed, is a professional CRM software, to automate as much as possible, to handle appointments and to get customers files fast with iPads straight from the treatment rooms.
‍
Then we rebranded the whole thing, they were using old branding since it was very small one room business, and it was not appealing.

When we done with branding, we planned a marketing strategy, partnered with an agency, which took care of PR, TV & radio interviews, copywriting and content, and I made the landing pages & digital assets.

And finally, a new website, an online home for Anuchka. Place where new customers understand why it so special and better then anything else they experienced, and for existing customers to view what else Anuchka offers.

The Results

Only one year after we started working together, Anuchka opened  another clinic in a prime location, which was designed on the new branding.  
‍
Not only it runs more than two rooms at a time, there are 7 working rooms in each clinic, so Anuchka's revenue and profit grew up in more than five times.

Started as a small town business, now it’s on its way to have a third clinic, in one of the most exclusive locations in Israel, can’t say more for now.
